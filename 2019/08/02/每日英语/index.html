[ɪ]

  • it [ɪt] 它
  • hit [hɪt] 打
  • ship [ʃɪp] 船🚢
  • sit [sɪt] 坐
  • fit [fɪt] 适合
  • give [gɪv] 给予
  • six [sɪks] 6
  • pig [pɪg] 猪🐷
  • bid [bɪd] 打招呼🙋

Sit down, please 请坐

This shirt doesn‘t fit me 这件衬衫👕不适合我

Give me a kiss 给我一个吻

下列情况[ɪ]需要发[i]音

[ɪ]在字尾的时候需要念[i]音

city [sɪtɪ] -> [sɪti] 城市
sleepy [slipɪ] -> [slipi] 想睡😪
Cindy [sɪndɪ] -> [sɪndi] 辛迪

[e] 双母音

  • lake [lek] 湖泊
  • make [mek] 制造
  • later [`let] 稍后

He lives by the lake

l’ll make coffee for you

l’ll be back later

⚠️[e]之后跟[n]的发音

  • plane [plen] 飞机
  • main [men] 主要
  • train [tren] 火车
  • saint [sent] 圣人

[ɛ] 相对[æ]嘴巴微开

  • pen [pɛn] 笔
  • let [lɛt] 让
  • bed [bɛd] 床
  • sex [sɛks] 性别
  • peg [pɛg] 木钉
  • set [sɛt] 放置
  • beg [bɛg] 乞求
  • lend [lɛnd] 借

You should go to bed

let me go

Can I use you pen

[æ] 相对[ɛ]嘴巴要长大

  • bad [bæd] 坏的
  • dad [dæd] 父亲👨
  • matter [`mæt] 事情
  • bag [bæg] 袋子
  • land [lænd] 土地
  • black [blæk] 黑的
  • cap [kæp] 帽子🎩

He is a bad boy.

My dad speaks English well.

What’s the matter with him?

  • car [car] 🚗车

  • law [lɔ] 法律
  • saw [sɔ] 锯子
  • hall [hɔl] 大厅
  • ball [bɔl] 球
  • kall [kɔl] 叫

[ɔr]

  • born [bɔrn] 出生
  • for [fɔr] 为了

[or] -> [ɔr]

four [for] -> [fɔr] 四
course [kors] -> [kɔrs] 课程
mourn [morn] -> [mɔrn] 哀悼
pour [por] -> [pɔr] 倾倒

[ɔɪ]

  • -oi- coin [kɔɪn] 硬币 noise [nɔɪz] 噪音 point [pɔɪnt] 重点
  • -oy- boy joy
